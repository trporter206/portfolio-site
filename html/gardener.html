A Personal Project

Gardener was inspired by the new trend of home gardening. As people are asked to stay indoors, the desire to bring the outdoors home became a need. But gardening isn't alwasys easy or simple, Gardener seeks to help those new to art of gardening by connecting a database with information on thousands of plants, so that they may grow as healthy and easily as possible in your home.
